Create an Adversary

Bruiser? Perks

Momentum

Reaction - Normal

When the Bruiser? makes a successful attack against a PC, you gain a Fear.
Ramp Up

Passive - Normal

You must spend a Fear to spotlight the Bruiser?. While spotlighted, they can make their standard attack against all targets within range.
Roar

Action - Soft

The Bruiser? roars in anger, preparing for its next strike. The next time the Bruiser? attacks, it gains an additional 1d4 to its attack roll.
Slow

Passive - Normal

When you spotlight the Bruiser? and they don’t have a token on their stat block, they can’t act yet. Place a token on their stat block and describe what they’re preparing to do. When you spotlight the Bruiser? and they have a token on their stat block, clear the token and they can act.
Terrifying

Passive - Normal

When the Bruiser? makes a successful attack, all PCs within Far range lose a Hope and you gain a Fear.

All Perks

AOE Condition

Action - Normal

Spend a Fear to make an attack against all targets within Very Close range. Targets the Bruiser? succeeds against become Restrained and Vulnerable <because of a thing that happened in the fiction>. A target can break free, ending both conditions, with a successful Trait Roll.
Activate Allies

Action - Normal

Spend X Fear to spotlight 1d4 allies. Attacks they make while spotlighted in this way deal half damage
Adrenaline Burst

Action - Normal

Once per scene, spend a Fear to clear 2 HP and 2 Stress.
Ambush

Action - Normal

While Hidden, make an attack against a target within <Range> range. On a success, deal <increased damage> physical damage.
Call Reinforcements

Action - Normal

Once per scene, mark a Stress to summon a <A different adversary>, which appears at Far/Very Far range.
Cleanse

Action - Soft

The Bruiser? clears a condition on themselves or someone else.
Cloaked

Action - Normal

Become Hidden until after the Bruiser?’s next attack. Attacks made while Hidden from this feature have advantage.
Countdown to (X)

Reaction - Normal

Countdown (Loop 1d6). When the <countdown activation condition>, activate the countdown. When it triggers, the Bruiser? <does something powerful (make an attack, force a Reaction Roll>. All targets that <It succeeds against/fail> Have a negative outcome.
Death Quake

Reaction - Normal

When the Bruiser? marks their last HP, the <insert fiction reason> ruptures in an explosion of force. All targets within Close range must succeed on an Instinct Reaction Roll or take 1d8+1 magic damage.
Direct Damage

Action - Normal

If the target or the adversary has a Condition damage dealt by the Bruiser? does direct damage.
Encourage

Action - Soft

The Bruiser? encourages one of their allies, giving them advantage on their next attack roll.
Flying

Passive - Normal

While flying, the Bruiser? gains a +2 bonus to their Difficulty.
Focus

Action - Soft

The Bruiser? focuses for their next attack, adding +X to the damage of their next attack if it hits.
Ground Slam

Action - Normal

Slam the ground, knocking all targets within Very Close range back to Far range. Each target knocked back this way must mark a Stress.
Group Attack

Action - Normal

Spend a Fear to choose a target and spotlight all Bruiser? within Close range of them. Those Minions move into Melee range of the target and make one shared attack roll. On a success, they deal <standard damage> physical damage each. Combine this damage.
Hit Multiple Targets

Reaction - Normal

Spend a Fear to make an attack against # targets within Far range. Targets the Bruiser? succeeds against take <reduced damage>
Horde

Passive - Normal

When the Bruiser? have marked half or more of their HP, their standard attack deals half physical damage instead
In Your Face

Passive - Normal

All targets within Melee range have disadvantage on attacks against targets other than the Bruiser?.
Merciless

Passive - Normal

When the Bruiser? is spotlighted, spotlight one additional ally without requiring a Fear to be spent to do so.
Minion (X)

Passive - Normal

The Bruiser? is defeated when they take any damage. For every X damage a PC deals to the Bruiser?, defeat an additional Minion within range the attack would succeed against.
Opportunist

Passive - Normal

When two or more adversaries are within Very Close range of a creature, all damage the Bruiser? deals to that creature is doubled.
Opportunity Shot

Reaction - Normal

When another adversary deals damage to a target within Far range of the Bruiser?, you can mark a Stress to add the <extra damage> to the damage roll.
Overwhelming Force

Passive - Normal

Targets who mark HP from the Bruiser?’s standard attack are knocked back to Very Close range
Pack Tactics

Passive - Normal

If the Bruiser? makes a successful standard attack and another Bruiser? is within Melee range of the target, deal <extra damage> physical damageinstead of their standard damage and you gain a Fear
Rally

Action - Soft

The Bruiser? rally together, gaining strength. They clear 1 HP or 1 Stress.
Rampage

Reaction - Normal

When the Bruiser? marks 2 or more HP, they can rampage. Move the Ogre to a point within Close range and deal 2x1d6+3 direct physical damage to all targets in their path.
Regeneration

Action - Normal

If the Bruiser? has any marked HP, spend a Fear to clear a HP and <do something else>.
Relentless (X)

Passive - Normal

The Bruiser? can be spotlighted up to X times per GM turn. Spend Fear as usual to spotlight them.
Retreat

Action - Soft

The Bruiser? retreats to a better position, disengaging from the PCs.
Surround

Action - Soft

The Bruiser? moves into a better position, surrounding the target.
Tactician

Action - Normal

When you spotlight the Bruiser?, mark a Stress to also spotlight two allies within Close range.
Too Many to Handle

Passive - Normal

When the Bruiser? is within Melee range of a creature and at least one other Bruiser? is within Close range, all attacks against that creature have advantage.
Help with the Bruiser
- Attacks can hit Major usually and Severe when rolling well. They tend to use a d10-d12 damage die.
- Features include attacks that hit multiple enemies and move PCs around the battlefield.
- Common Features: Ramp Up, Momentum.
- Common Experiences: Crusher, Charger, Intimidation, Throw
